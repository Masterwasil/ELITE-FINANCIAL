Service Overview

In an increasingly regulated business environment, audits are more than just a statutory obligation—they are a tool for building trust with stakeholders, investors, and banks. At Elite Financial, our Audit & Assurance practice focuses on delivering high-quality, independent audits that adhere strictly to Indian Accounting Standards (Ind AS) and Standards on Auditing (SA).

Our methodology is risk-based, meaning we focus our efforts on the areas of highest risk to your business. This ensures efficiency and relevance in our reporting, providing you with recommendations that improve internal controls and financial reporting quality.

Our Audit Methodology

01

Planning & Risk Assessment

Understanding the business environment, identifying key risk areas, and designing an audit strategy tailored to the entity's size and complexity.

02

Control Testing

Evaluating the design and operating effectiveness of internal controls to determine the extent of substantive testing required.

03

Substantive Testing

Detailed verification of transactions, account balances, and disclosures using data analytics and sampling techniques.

04

Reporting & Value Add

Issuing the audit report along with a management letter highlighting control weaknesses and operational improvement opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between statutory and internal audit?

Statutory audit is mandatory by law (Companies Act) to report on the financial state of affairs to shareholders. Internal audit is management-driven, focusing on improving operations, risk management, and internal controls.

Do you conduct stock audits for banks?

Yes, we are empanelled with several banks to conduct stock and receivable audits to verify the security and valuation of assets hypothecated to the bank.

How do you help with SOP implementation?

We study your current processes ("As-Is"), identify gaps or inefficiencies, design robust Standard Operating Procedures ("To-Be"), and conduct walkthroughs to ensure they are practically implemented by your team.

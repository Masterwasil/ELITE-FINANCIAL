Our Approach

A Structured
Process for Every Mandate

Every assignment is executed through a predictable yet flexible framework. Clear stages, clear ownership and clear timelines ensure that you always know where we are in the engagement and what comes next.

Typical Engagement Flow

  1. Discovery call & requirement mapping
  2. Scope, fees & timelines finalisation
  3. Data collection & onboarding
  4. Execution with periodic reviews
  5. Final sign-off & ongoing support

*The exact flow may vary slightly depending on the nature of the assignment – for example, audits, tax matters, advisory projects or Virtual CFO mandates.

Five-Stage Engagement Model

The following five stages apply across most services – whether GST compliance, statutory audits, income-tax advisory or strategic assignments. This brings discipline and transparency to every engagement.

01

Discover

We understand your business model, entity structure, current systems and immediate concerns in depth. This stage is focused on listening and clarifying expectations.

  • • 30–60 minute discovery discussion
  • • Review of key reports and filings
  • • Capture of key risk and pain areas
02

Diagnose

We analyse the data and documents to identify compliance gaps, control weaknesses and improvement opportunities.

  • • Risk and gap analysis
  • • Prioritised list of issues
  • • Initial high-level recommendations
03

Design

We convert the diagnosis into a practical plan – clearly defining scope, timelines, deliverables and responsibilities in a written proposal.

  • • Detailed scope of work
  • • Fee structure & payment milestones
  • • Responsibility matrix (client vs. firm)
04

Deliver

We execute as per the agreed plan – filings, audits, reports or advisory notes – sharing interim updates at key milestones.

  • • Periodic review calls
  • • Drafts and final deliverables
  • • Robust documentation and working papers
05

Review

After completion, we conduct a review session to summarise key findings, learning points and recommended next steps.

  • • Management debrief
  • • 3–6 month action roadmap
  • • Option for ongoing retainer support

Client Onboarding Checklist

Once an engagement is confirmed, we follow a structured onboarding process. This ensures information is organised and our team can start work smoothly.

KYC & Engagement Letter

We complete basic KYC documentation, sign an engagement letter and, where required, execute NDAs so that responsibilities and confidentiality are clearly defined.

Data Room & Access

A secure shared folder or data room is set up for accounting data, returns, bank statements, agreements and other supporting documents.

Single Point of Contact

Both sides nominate a single point of contact so that communication is streamlined and decisions are taken quickly.

Timeline & Milestones

We share a calendar of important dates, filing due dates and review meetings right at the start of the engagement.

Engagement Models

Depending on the nature of work, we use different commercial models:

  • Fixed-fee mandates: Clearly defined scope and timelines – for example, statutory audits, project reports or one-time reviews.
  • Retainer model: Monthly or quarterly fee for ongoing support – such as Virtual CFO, routine GST/Income-tax compliance and advisory on call.
  • Hybrid structure: A base retainer plus a success-linked component – typically used for fund-raising, special representations or large refund matters.

All commercials are discussed transparently before we begin. There are no hidden charges or surprise billing.

Communication Cadence

Clear communication is the foundation of a successful compliance and advisory relationship. We follow a defined rhythm of updates throughout the year.

  • WhatsApp & Email Updates

    Due dates, payment challans and acknowledgement copies are shared promptly so that you never miss a deadline.

  • Monthly / Quarterly Reviews

    Regular review calls where we discuss key numbers, open issues and upcoming actions – especially for retainer and Virtual CFO clients.

  • Standardised Reporting

    MIS dashboards, audit observations and tax position notes are shared in a consistent format, making it easier for management and boards to take decisions.

Quality & Review Mechanism

Every major deliverable passes through a partner-level review. In addition, internal quality control steps are built into the workflow:

  • • Dual review for all critical tax computations and returns
  • • Peer review of audit working papers
  • • Checklists aligned with ICAI and regulatory requirements
  • • Regular internal training on new laws and amendments

The outcome is fewer notices, stronger defence during scrutiny and greater confidence in the numbers that go to your stakeholders.

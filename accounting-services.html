Your Virtual Finance Department

The Accounting Cycle

01

Data Collection

We set up a seamless flow for you to share invoices, bank statements, and expense receipts digitally (via Drive, email, or portal).

02

Recording & Compliance

Our team records transactions, applying correct TDS/GST rates and accounting standards (Ind AS/GAAP).

03

Review & Close

Month-end procedures including bank reconciliations, accruals, and a senior-level review to ensure data integrity.

04

Reporting

Delivery of the monthly financial pack (P&L, Balance Sheet, Ageing Reports) offering insights into business performance.

Common Questions

Which accounting software do you support?

We are proficient in all major platforms including Tally Prime, Zoho Books, QuickBooks Online, and Xero. We can work on your existing license via remote access or cloud login.

How often is the data updated?

This depends on the volume and your requirement. We offer daily, weekly, or fortnightly bookkeeping cycles to ensure your reports are always timely.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We adhere to strict data privacy protocols. We sign NDAs and use secure, encrypted channels for all data transfers. Access is restricted to authorized team members only.

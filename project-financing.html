Common Questions

What is a Detailed Project Report (DPR)?

A DPR is a comprehensive document that covers the technical, economic, financial, and managerial aspects of a proposed project. It serves as the blueprint for the bank to assess the viability and risk of lending.

Do you guarantee loan sanction?

While no consultant can ethically guarantee a sanction (as it is the bank's prerogative), our structured approach, realistic projections, and professional presentation significantly maximize the probability of approval.

How is Working Capital Limit calculated?

Working capital is typically assessed using the Turnover Method (for limits up to ₹5 Cr) or the Maximum Permissible Bank Finance (MPBF) method, which analyzes your holding periods for stock and receivables.

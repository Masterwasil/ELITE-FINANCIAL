Common Questions

Should I register as an LLP or Private Limited Company?

An LLP is cost-effective with fewer compliance requirements, making it ideal for professional services or small businesses. A Private Limited Company is preferred if you plan to raise venture capital or issue ESOPs, as it offers a distinct separation between ownership and management.

What is Due Diligence?

Due diligence is a comprehensive appraisal of a business undertaken by a prospective buyer or investor. We examine financial records, legal compliance, and tax liabilities to establish the true value and risks associated with the deal.

Why do I need a Shareholders' Agreement?

While the Articles of Association (AOA) define general rules, a Shareholders' Agreement (SHA) specifically protects the rights of owners. It clarifies exit strategies, dispute resolution mechanisms, and rights regarding the transfer of shares.

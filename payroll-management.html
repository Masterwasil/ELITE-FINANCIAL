Common Questions

Is PF registration mandatory for my company?

Provident Fund (PF) registration is generally mandatory for organizations with 20 or more employees. However, many startups register voluntarily to offer better social security benefits to attract talent.

How do you handle employee data confidentiality?

We use secure, encrypted channels for data transfer and storage. Access to salary data is restricted to specific authorized personnel within our firm to ensure absolute confidentiality.

Can you help structure salary to minimize tax for employees?

Yes. We advise on restructuring CTC to include tax-efficient components like HRA, LTA, Meal Coupons, and NPS contributions, maximizing in-hand salary while staying compliant with IT laws.

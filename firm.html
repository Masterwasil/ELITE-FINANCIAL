Legacy

From a Single Room Practice to a Multi-Disciplinary Firm

What began as a modest proprietorship practice in a small office near Vijay Nagar has grown into a respected mid-sized firm servicing clients across manufacturing, healthcare, FMCG, technology, real estate and not-for-profit sectors.

Over the years, we have consciously chosen depth over scale. Rather than chasing volumes, we have built long-term relationships with promoter families, boards and CXOs who view us as their first call for financial decisions.

Our journey has been guided by three non-negotiables – integrity, technical excellence and responsiveness. Every engagement, from a basic tax return to a cross-border restructuring, is executed with the same seriousness.

Our Philosophy

  • Client-Centric, Not Product-Centric
    We recommend structures and strategies that are genuinely right for the client’s risk profile, not what is fashionable or fee-driven.
  • Substance Over Form
    We believe compliance should align with commercial reality. Our advice always considers the long-term sustainability of any structure.
  • Technology with a Human Touch
    Automation and analytics are powerful tools, but we never lose sight of judgement, experience and personal access to partners.
  • Ethics Above All
    We would rather lose an assignment than compromise on professional ethics or regulatory expectations.

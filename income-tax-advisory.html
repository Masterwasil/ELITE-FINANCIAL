Service Overview

Income tax laws in India are dynamic and require constant vigilance. At Elite Financial, we provide comprehensive direct tax advisory services that go beyond mere compliance. We work with you throughout the year to plan your taxes effectively, rather than just managing them at the end of the financial year.

Whether you are a corporate entity looking to restructure, or an HNI planning for succession, our approach focuses on legality, transparency, and optimization. We handle the complexities of the Income Tax Act so you can focus on growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you help with responding to high-value transaction notices?

Yes, we handle responses to notices regarding high-value transactions (AIR/SFT), cash deposits, or discrepancies in AIS/TIS data.

Can you assist with capital gains on property sale?

Absolutely. We advise on calculating capital gains, claiming exemptions (e.g., Section 54/54EC), and reinvestment options to save tax on property sales.

What is succession planning?

Succession planning involves structuring your assets and business ownership to ensure smooth transfer to the next generation with minimal tax impact and legal disputes, often using Wills or Family Trusts.

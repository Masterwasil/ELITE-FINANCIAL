Essential for Digital Compliance

In the modern regulatory landscape, a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) is the digital equivalent of a physical signature. It is critical for validating the identity of the signer and ensuring the integrity of electronic documents.

Elite Financial streamlines the entire DSC lifecycle. Whether you need a Class 3 DSC for e-Tendering or a simple renewal for Income Tax filing, we handle the KYC, USB token configuration, and portal registration so you can sign documents instantly.

Common Questions

What is the difference between Class 2 and Class 3 DSC?

As per new CCA guidelines, Class 2 DSC has been discontinued. Class 3 DSC is now the standard for all purposes, including e-Tendering, GST, Income Tax, and ROC filings, offering the highest level of security.

Can I use the same DSC for my personal and company filings?

An "Individual" DSC is issued to a person and can be used for personal tax filings and as a director in a company. However, for e-Tendering, you may require an "Organization" DSC which includes the company name.

What is the validity of a DSC?

DSCs are typically issued with a validity of 2 or 3 years. We recommend opting for 3 years to minimize renewal hassles. You cannot extend validity; a new certificate must be issued upon expiry.

Common Questions

When is Form 15CA and 15CB required?

Form 15CA is required for most foreign remittances to report the payment to the Tax Department. Form 15CB is a certificate from a Chartered Accountant confirming that appropriate tax (TDS) has been deducted before sending money abroad. It is generally required for payments that are taxable in India.

What is DTAA benefit?

Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA) is a treaty between two countries. It allows a taxpayer to avoid paying tax on the same income in both countries. You can either get an exemption in one country or claim credit for tax paid in the other country.

Do NRIs have to file Income Tax Returns in India?

Yes, if an NRI's income earned or accrued in India (like rent, interest, or capital gains) exceeds the basic exemption limit (₹2.5 Lakhs), they must file an ITR. It is also recommended to file returns to claim tax refunds if TDS was deducted.

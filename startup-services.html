Common Questions

When should I create an ESOP pool?

Ideally, an ESOP pool is created at the incorporation stage or just before your first external funding round. This ensures that the dilution hits the founders and not the incoming investors, which is a standard expectation.

What is the difference between Pre-money and Post-money valuation?

Pre-money valuation is the value of your company before you receive the investment. Post-money valuation is the Pre-money value plus the amount of new cash injected. This distinction is crucial for calculating dilution.

Do I need a Valuation Report for seed funding?

Yes. Under the Companies Act and Income Tax Act (Angel Tax provisions), a registered valuation report is often mandatory when issuing shares at a premium, even for seed rounds, to justify the share price to authorities.
